About Me

Hey, my name is Jason Lien and I'm a graphic designer pivoting my career towards UI Design. I have a holistic design approach and enjoy human interaction with design. Any project from a logo on a lapel pin to an e-commerce website, I focus on how a human can interact with my work. I believe that communication and setting expectations on the schedule of deliverables are important when dealing with a variety of projects. I aim to keep learning and pushing myself down this new career path. I am ready to join an innovative team that puts creative problem-solving as a priority.
